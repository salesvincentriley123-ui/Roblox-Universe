Famous Outfit Genres 2026

1) Acubi

• Popular for its clean layered clothing,neutral colors and chill aesthetic

• Usually uses baggy pants,oversized hoodies and simple accesories

• One of the biggest modern Roblox fashion trends in 2026

2) Y2K Streetwear

• Inspired by early 2000s fashion mixed modern streetwear

• Uses baggy jeans, flashy jackets, chains and colorful accesories

• Very common in hangout and avatar rating games

3) Rich / Limited Flex

• Focuses on expensive items like Korblox, Headless, Valks, etc

• Meant to show rarity and roblox wealth

• Often paired with dark or simple clothing to make accesories stand out

4) Cartoony Brainrot Avatar

• Uses funny faces, meme hats, silly body proportions and joke ugc

• Inspired by viral Roblox humor and TikTok trends

• Popular in chaotic games and social servers

5) Softie

• Cute and friendly aesthetic using pastel colors and cozy outfits

• Common items include oversized sweaters, fluffy hats and soft accesories

• Popular for casual roleplay games

6) Dark Goth

• Dark clothing, black accesories, chains and dramatic styling

• Inspired by gothic and alternative fashion

• Often combined with edgy animations

7) D4SHIE

• A mix of Acubi, Y2K and soft streetwear together

• Always has the d4shie ugc

• Cheap and affordable outfit

8) Anime Inspired

• Based on anime characters and Japanese fashion trends

• Uses unique hairstyles, swords, school uniforms and anime faces

• Extremely popular due to anime games on Roblox
