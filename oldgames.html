Old Games in Roblox

1) Natural Disaster Survival

Made by: Stickmasterluke

One of the most iconic roblox games ever

Survive floods,tornadoes,earthquakes and more

Simple but still really fun with friends

2) Work at a Pizza Place

Made by: Dued1

Classic roleplay/j*b game

You can cook pizzas, deliver orders or manage the restaurant

Very nostalgic and still active today

3) Murder Mystery 2

Made by: Nikilis

One murder, one sheriff and everyone else survives

Huge classic in roblox history (still active today)

Still one of the most played older games

4) Hide and Seek Extreme

Made by: Tim7775

Giant-map hide and seek game

Everyone else hides while one seeker hunts everyone down

One of the most nostalgic roblox games ever

5) Epic Minigames

Made by: TypicalType

Escape prison or stop prisoners as a cop

Great for casual fun and old-school Roblox energy

Easy to play even if you’re new

6) Prison Life

Made by: Aesthethical

Escape prison or stop prisoners as a cop

One of the biggest PvP games on Roblox

Chaotic but legendary

7) Speed Run 4

Made by: Vurse

Fast obby game with iconic music and levels

Very old Roblox aesthetic

Suprisingly addicting

8) Roblox High School

Made by: Cindering

Attend classes, customize your house, drive cars and hang out with friends

Famous for its classic 2010s Roblox vibe and roleplay community

Helped inspire many modern Roblox life/roleplay games
